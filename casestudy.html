Overview

MyFlix is a web application developed using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js). It offers users access to information about movies, directors, and genres. Users can create an account, update their personal details, and curate a personalized list of favorite movies.

Purpose & Context

This is a personal project I created as part of my Full-Stack Web Development course at CareerFoundry. The objective was to develop skills in both front-end and back-end development. This included creating databases and building the front end using React and Angular. Through this project, I aimed to gain holistic proficiency in full-stack application development.

Duration

Creating the client-side took about twice as long as creating the server-side. I wanted to make sure I had a good grasp on how React and Redux work and achieve the desired result.

My Journey into Web 🌐 Development


Ever since my first encounter with a computer running Microsoft Windows 3.11 at age 9, I vividly remember entering code lines in DOS and playing Minesweeper and Solitaire. This early fascination with technology led me to pursue a computer-led high school education and later a Bachelor’s degree in Media and Communication from the University of Stockholm

While working with technical solutions in SaaS, I discovered a renewed passion for technology. This role, combined with my experience in customer service, account management, and sales, allowed me to find a niche in web development where I could blend my technical expertise with my user-centric approach.

Motivated by this renewed passion, I pursued further education in full-stack web development, completing courses and certifications that honed my skills. Through 500+ hours of hands-on training and projects, under the guidance of industry-leading tutors and mentors, I have developed a robust technical skill set and enhanced my problem-solving abilities, patience, and determination.

As I advance in my career, I seek opportunities that leverage both my technical skills and user-centric perspectives to optimize solutions, solve complex problems, and drive customer satisfaction.
